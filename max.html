How to find the maximum value?

Finding the maximum value involves identifying the largest number in a set. To find the maximum value:

1. Identify the numbers you want to compare.

2. Enter the numbers separated by commas.

3. Click on "Calculate" to get the maximum value.

What is the maximum value?

The maximum value is the largest number in a set of numbers. For example, the maximum of 5, 3, and 8 is 8.
